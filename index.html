My name is Michael Wilson

Hi everyone! I'm Michael Wilson, and I’m from Toronto, Canada. With over 5 years of hands-on experience in game development, I’ve honed my skills in Project Management, Game Design, and Game Production, allowing me to lead and direct a wide range of projects from concept to completion. I excel in implementing Agile methodologies, ensuring team productivity, clear communication, and efficient workflows that consistently meet deadlines and surpass user expectations. My diverse portfolio of game titles showcases my ability to balance creativity with organization, driving projects to deliver immersive and polished player experiences.

My passion for games goes beyond development—I’m deeply inspired by the way games combine storytelling, art, and interactivity to create unforgettable moments. This passion fuels my commitment to employing cutting-edge production strategies and fostering collaboration across teams to bring innovative ideas to life. I thrive in dynamic environments where I can tackle challenges, problem-solve, and contribute to building experiences that resonate with players.

Academically, I graduated from Humber College with honours (4.0 GPA) in Game Programming, and I’m currently pursuing my Bachelor's Degree in Game Development & Interactive Media at Ontario Tech University. With a strong foundation in production and a dedication to continuous learning, I’m excited to keep evolving as a leader in the game development industry and help create games that inspire and captivate.
